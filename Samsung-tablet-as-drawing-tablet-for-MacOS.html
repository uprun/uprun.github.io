Question:

How to use Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite as a drawing tablet for MacOS ( MacBook Air M1)?

Answer:

Use Google Chrome Remote Desktop app both on the tablet and on the laptop.

[date of post 2023-02-11 23:26 CET]

My thoughts:

This is the fastest out of the shelf solution that I could find, yet it is not ideal.

Samsung is building their own walled garden (or ecosystem of products) just like Apple does. I admire both companies for their products.

This blog (Q&A entry) was written on MacBook Air M1 2020 and I use Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite 2020 as my tablet for small drawings and for reading books.
